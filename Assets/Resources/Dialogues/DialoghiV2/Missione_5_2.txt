The Geiger counter is used to analyze the radiation level of a location.
To turn it on, press [TASTO DELLA CONSOLE UTILIZZATA/2].
When the Geiger counter light is red, the radiations are too high to set up a laboratory.
When the Geiger counter light is yellow, the radiations are slightly less oppressive, but it still won’t be enough to set up a laboratory.
When the Geiger counter light is green, the place is ideal to set up a laboratory.
